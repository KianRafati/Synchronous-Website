About Synchronous 2023

Synchronous is a newly-formed annual contest held by Resana scientific association which addresses students majoring in electrical and computer engineering and related sciences. Participants have to team up and compete in projects requiring practical and hands-on applications of their knowledge. These projects are defined based on cutting-edge technological advances of highly-ranked scientific institutes and industrial needs of incorporations around the globe.

Join us for a day full of exciting activities, inspiring presentations, and networking opportunities. Participants will have a chance to explore the latest advancements in electrical engineering and gain practical experience in a competitive environment.
